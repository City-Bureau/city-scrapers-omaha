PLUMBING EXAM SCHEDULE

Plumbing Board Codes, Forms, Policies, and Continuing Education Opportunities

The Plumbing Board is a seven-member board that is empowered to adopt rules and regulations pertaining to the construction and inspection of plumbing and sewer systems.

This board also conducts examinations for master and journeyman plumbers.

Plumbing examinations are given four times yearly, in February, May, August, and November. Applications for testing are reviewed at the meeting before the scheduled test date.

Parking and Entry to Civic Center

  • The parking garage at 19th and Harney Streets is available for parking but everyone is required to enter at the Farnam Street entrance which is the only entrance accessible without an employee scan badge.
