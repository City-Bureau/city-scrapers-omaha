Zoning Board of Appeals

PLANNING DEPARTMENT

 

Sign up for updates

To receive email notifications of changes to this page, please sign up for RSS Feeds in the link above.

The Zoning Board of Appeals is a quasi-judicial board made up of five members and two alternates. 

This Board holds a public hearing on the second Thursday of each month at 1:00 p.m. in the Civic Center's Legislative Chambers (1819 Farnam Street) to consider:

  • applications for appeals to the use
  • site development
  • landscaping
  • parking and signage regulations in the zoning ordinance
  • any interpretation of the regulations by the Planning Department staff

Any challenge to a decision by this Board must be appealed to the District Court.  

The current agenda and exhibits can be viewed on our monthly Zoning Board of Appeals Web Map

Application Filing Fee Schedule:

$150 - project cost under $25,000
$250 - project cost $25,000-$100,000
$445 - project cost $100,00 and over
$445 - miscellaneous applications
$985 - use waivers 

All applications must add the 8% Technology and Training Fee to the fee listed above.

Parking and Entry to Civic Center

  • The parking garage at 19th and Harney Streets is available for parking but everyone is required to enter at the Farnam Street entrance which is the only entrance accessible without an employee scan badge.
