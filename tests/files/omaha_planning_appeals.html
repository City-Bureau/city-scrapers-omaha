The Administrative Board of Appeals is a five-member board. The board handles all appeals concerning conflicts regarding the granting, issuance, denial, revocation, or cancellation of any permit or license by any official or department.  This board meets on the fourth Monday of each month.

To request a hearing before this board, please write a letter addressed to:

Administrative Board of Appeals
ATTN: Clinette Ingram
City Planning Department
1819 Farnam Street Suite 1100
Omaha, NE 68183

In this letter, state the reason you are requesting a hearing before the Administrative Board of Appeals (for example, you were denied a special dance permit, leased right-of-way parking permit, ordered to abate a nuisance, etc.). Send this letter, along with your letter of denial from the City and any attachments that you wish the Board to see to the Planning Department.

Notices of meetings are sent to all parties. Applicants are required to attend the meeting to explain their reasons for the appeal. Notice to appear will be sent to you via certified mail.

The fee to appear before this board is $50.00 for an appeal regarding firearms or firearm safety classes and $35.00 for all other appeals. Appeals filed under Section 11-115 of the Omaha Municipal Code must be filed within five days of the receipt of written notice of the entry of an order concerning the issuance, denial, suspension, cancellation, or revocation of any permit provided for in Chapter 11 of the Omaha Municipal Code. All other appeals must be filed within ten calendar days.
